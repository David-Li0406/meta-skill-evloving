# Sales Letter and Copywriting Skill

This skill combines advanced copywriting techniques with a structured approach to creating sales letters, landing pages, and marketing copy.

## When to Use This Skill

Utilize this skill in the following scenarios:
- To create a sales letter or landing page.
- When you need to write step emails or marketing newsletters.
- To generate high-converting copy for products or services.

## Key Copywriting Techniques

### 1. Interactive Copy
Engage readers by prompting them to think and arrive at their own conclusions, minimizing the feeling of being sold to.

### 2. Emotional Rollercoaster
Utilize a cycle of fear and hope to captivate the reader's attention and keep them engaged.

### 3. Fusion of Numbers and Stories
Combine specific numerical data with relatable narratives to enhance credibility and connection.

## Structure of a Sales Letter

1. **Head Copy** (Catchphrase)
2. **Problem Recognition**
3. **Solution Presentation**
4. **Benefits and Future Vision**
5. **Evidence and Testimonials**
6. **Product Details**
7. **Price Presentation**
8. **Bonuses**
9. **Guarantee and Support**
10. **Call to Action (CTA)**
11. **Postscript (P.S.)**

## Customization Based on Product Price

### High-Priced Products (Over 300,000 yen)
- Build authority through a four-layer structure (achievements, experience, knowledge, personality).
- Use explanatory phrases (e.g., "it is" or "it means") 30% of the time.
- Offer a 90-day money-back guarantee and installment payment options.

### Mid-Priced Products (100,000 - 300,000 yen)
- Include at least three specific success stories.
- Quantify cost-effectiveness (e.g., ROI of 10,000%).
- Use comparative storytelling (traditional vs. your product).

### Low-Priced Products (Under 100,000 yen)
- Use short sentences (52% of the text) and exclamatory sentences (12%) for pacing.
- Emphasize immediate usability.
- Increase the frequency of bullet points.

## Guidelines for Writing

- Focus on emotionally compelling expressions.
- Present logical reasoning for price justification.
- Clearly articulate benefits.
- Ensure the CTA is direct and unambiguous.

## Checklist for Sales Letter Creation

### Pre-Writing
- [ ] Define the target audience.
- [ ] Choose a pricing strategy based on product category.
- [ ] Prepare five compelling stories.
- [ ] Gather evidence (images, case studies).
- [ ] Organize authority structure.

### Writing
- [ ] Maintain a balance of sentence lengths.
- [ ] Use interactive questions to engage readers.
- [ ] Ensure emotional peaks and valleys are present.

### Post-Writing
- [ ] Read aloud to check for flow.
- [ ] Ensure clarity and absence of errors.
- [ ] Confirm that the CTA is prominent.
