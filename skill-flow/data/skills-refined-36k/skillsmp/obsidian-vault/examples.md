# Obsidian Vault Skill - Example Queries

This file contains examples of queries that should trigger the Obsidian Vault skill.

## People and Meeting Queries

### Finding Last Meeting with Someone
- "When was the last time I was in a meeting with Kashish?"
- "When did I last meet with Pahwa?"
- "What was the most recent meeting with Gabriella Luna?"
- "Show me the last meeting with Royce"

### Meeting Content Queries
- "What did we discuss with Kashish about devices?"
- "What did Kashish say in the standup?"
- "What was discussed in the Green Standup yesterday?"
- "Show me notes from the Aruba sync meeting"

## Topic and Decision Queries

### Finding Decisions
- "What did we decide to do about the horizontal auto scaler for SIC?"
