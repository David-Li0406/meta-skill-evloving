## Components of Writing Skills

### 1. Writing Style Guidelines

- **Voice and Tone**: Guidelines on maintaining a consistent voice and tone.
- **Specificity and Evidence**: Rules for providing clear and supported content.
- **Banned Words and Phrases**: A list of terms to avoid for clarity and professionalism.
- **Punctuation and Formatting**: Standards for punctuation usage and document formatting.

### 2. Testing Methodology

- **When to Test Skills**: Guidelines on the appropriate times to conduct tests.
- **Writing Pressure Scenarios**: Techniques for creating realistic testing scenarios.
- **Key Elements of Good Scenarios**: What makes a testing scenario effective.
- **Testing Setup**: Instructions for preparing the testing environment.
- **VERIFY GREEN Process**: Steps to ensure tests pass successfully.
- **Meta-Testing**: Strategies for evaluating the effectiveness of tests.
- **Complete Worked Examples**: Detailed examples demonstrating the application of the methodology.
