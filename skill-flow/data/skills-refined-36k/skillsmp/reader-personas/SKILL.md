---
name: reader-personas
description: Simulate multiple reader personas to score a chapter (爽点/困惑/信息负荷) and propose actionable alternatives.
---

## When to use
- Before publishing a chapter
- When the author wants to optimize pacing, clarity, and payoff strength

## Personas (v1 suggested set)
- **爽文核心读者**：追求爽点密度、节奏快、升级清晰
- **推理/悬疑读者**：关注伏笔、逻辑闭环、因果链
- **世界观设定控**：关注规则一致、术语准确、设定引用清晰
- **情感共鸣读者**：关注人物动机、情绪推进、关系弧线

## Scoring dimensions (0-10)
- **爽点强度**
- **困惑指数**（越低越好）
- **信息负荷**（越低越不累，但太低也可能太水）
- **设定清晰度**
- **情绪连续性**

## Output
- `reader_persona_report.md`: scores + short rationale + concrete suggestions
- Must include 2-3 alternative routes:
  - keep plot, improve clarity
  - keep clarity, increase payoff
  - aggressive rewrite (if necessary)
