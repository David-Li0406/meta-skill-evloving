Luxury bathroom renovation

Location Brisbane, QLD · Year 2024 · Type Residential bathroom
Full bathroom installation

Overview

Complete bathroom renovation for a high-end residential property in Brisbane. The project required precision waterproofing, large-format porcelain wall tiles, and custom-cut floor tiles with perfect falls to a linear drain.

Working with the client's interior designer, we achieved a seamless finish with zero lip transitions and grout lines aligned across all surfaces.

Wall tiling detail

600×1200mm porcelain tiles with 2mm grout lines

Niche detail

Custom niche with waterproofing and tile alignment

The challenge

The bathroom featured an existing concrete slab that was significantly out of level, requiring extensive screed work to achieve proper falls. The client wanted a completely seamless look with the shower area flush to the main floor level.

Additionally, the large-format tiles needed to align perfectly across multiple walls and the floor, with all cuts concealed in the least visible areas.

Process & execution

01 / Preparation

Substrate correction

Screeded the entire floor area to achieve correct falls, with the shower area sloping perfectly to a central linear drain. All levels checked with a laser and verified at multiple stages.

02 / Waterproofing

Multi-coat membrane

Applied waterproofing membrane to specification, with particular attention to corners, penetrations, and the shower area. All work independently inspected and certified before tiling.

03 / Tiling

Precision installation

Dry-laid all tiles first to finalize the layout and minimize visible cuts. Used leveling system throughout to ensure perfectly flat surfaces across all large-format tiles.

04 / Finishing

Grout & seal

Applied premium epoxy grout for durability and easy maintenance. All tiles sealed with high-quality penetrating sealer. Final clean and handover with maintenance instructions.

Completed bathroom

Finished bathroom with seamless floor transitions and precision grout lines

Specifications

Wall tiles 600×1200mm porcelain
Floor tiles 600×600mm porcelain
Grout 2mm epoxy grout
Waterproofing Multi-coat membrane system
Adhesive Premium flexible adhesive
Duration 3 weeks

The result

A flawless, luxury bathroom installation that exceeded the client's expectations. The seamless floor transition, perfectly aligned tiles, and precision grout lines showcase the attention to detail that defines our work.

The client has since referred us to three other properties in their portfolio.
