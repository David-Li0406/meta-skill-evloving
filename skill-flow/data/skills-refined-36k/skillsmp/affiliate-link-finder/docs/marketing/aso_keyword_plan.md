# ASO Keyword Plan: ADHD Supplement App

## Primary Keywords (Focus: Core Intent)
| Keyword | Intent | Priority |
|---------|--------|----------|
| ADHD Manager | High | Critical |
| Supplement Tracker | High | Critical |
| Focus Booster | Medium | High |
| Vitamin Log | Low | Medium |
| ADHD Productivity | High | High |

## Long-Tail Keywords (Focus: Niche Conversion)
- "Smart supplement stack for ADHD"
- "Track morning focus vitamins"
- "ADHD habit tracker with reminders"
- "Pill identifier for focus supplements"

- **Keywords to Target**: "L-Theanine tracker", "Caffeine & L-Theanine stack", "Omega-3 for focus".
- **Visual Strategy**: Highlight "No Clutter" and "ADHD-Friendly" in screenshots. Use high-contrast colors (Gold/Black) to stand out from clinical blue apps.

## Localization Plan
- **Phase 1**: English (US/UK/AU)
- **Phase 2**: Spanish (focused on Latin American market growing in ADHD awareness).
