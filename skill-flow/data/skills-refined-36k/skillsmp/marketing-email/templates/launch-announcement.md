# Template: Launch Announcement Email

## Purpose
Drive action for a specific offering. Convert interested subscribers.

## Best rhetoric devices
- **Antithesis** for before/after contrasts
- **Tricolon** for listing benefits
- **Anaphora** for building momentum

## Structure

```
Subject: [Clear announcement with urgency or value]

[HOOK: The transformation or opportunity]

[PROBLEM: What life looks like without this]

[SOLUTION: What this offering provides]

[PROOF: Why you're the right person / results others have gotten]

[DETAILS: What's included - bullet points]

[OFFER: Price, timing, bonuses if any]

[URGENCY: Why act now - deadline, limited spots, price increase]

[CTA: Clear, single call to action]

[Your name]
```

## Subject Line Examples
- "The [Offering Name] opens Monday"
- "Doors close in 48 hours"
- "Your invitation: [Offering Name] [Year]"
- "It's time"
- "The wait is over"

## Email Sequence for Launch

### Pre-launch (Day -7)
- Announce launch is coming
- Build anticipation
- No CTA yet

### Open (Day 0)
- Full announcement (template above)
- Clear CTA

### Social proof (Day 2)
- Student/customer stories / testimonials
- Specific results
- CTA

### FAQ / Objections (Day 4)
- Common questions answered
- Overcome hesitations
- CTA

### Final reminder (Day 6)
- Last chance messaging
- Urgency (doors closing)
- Strong CTA

### Closed (Day 7)
- Thank those who joined
- When next opportunity is
- What to do in meantime

## Voice notes
- More direct than newsletters - there's an ask
- Still personal, not corporate
- Specifics build trust (exact numbers, named companies if possible)
- Urgency must be real, not manufactured
- Offer the "reply for honest advice" out
