# Taiyo Style Email Marketing Skill

## Overview

This skill applies the Taiyo style of copywriting, which has generated significant revenue, to create effective email marketing campaigns, including step emails and sales letters. It utilizes a combination of emotional triggers, educational elements, and structured sequences to enhance open rates, click rates, and conversion rates.

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- Create a step email sequence using the Taiyo style.
- Write high-converting sales letters or email campaigns.
- Develop engaging content for email marketing that maximizes reader engagement.

## Taiyo Style Framework

### Step Email Structure (Recommended 7-14 Emails)

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│           Taiyo Style Step Email Structure                 │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                             │
│  Day 0: Welcome + Shocking Promise                          │
│       ↓                                                     │
│  Day 1-2: Deepening Problem Awareness (Fear Trigger)       │
│       ↓                                                     │
│  Day 3-4: Hinting at Solutions (Hope Trigger)              │
│       ↓                                                     │
│  Day 5-6: Building Credibility (Social Proof)              │
│       ↓                                                     │
│  Day 7: Sales Launch (Urgency Trigger)                      │
│       ↓                                                     │
│  Day 8-10: Follow-up Sales (Scarcity + Loss Aversion)      │
│                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Key Elements of the Taiyo Style

1. **Emotional Rollercoaster**: Alternate between fear and hope to engage readers emotionally.
2. **Educational Components**: Incorporate six educational elements to guide readers through the decision-making process.
3. **Data-Driven Storytelling**: Use specific numbers and relatable stories to enhance credibility and connection.

### Email Templates

#### Day 0: Welcome Email

**Purpose**: Build anticipation and establish a habit of opening emails.

**Structure**:
```
Subject: [Shocking Number] + [Promise]
1. Thank you + Introduction (2-3 lines)
2. Shocking Promise (with specific numbers)
3. Why you are receiving this
4. What to expect in future emails
5. Teaser for tomorrow's content
6. P.S. (Planting urgency)
```

#### Day 1: Problem Awareness

**Purpose**: Clarify the reader's pain points.

**Structure**:
```
Subject: [Reader's Problem] + [Question]
1. Start with empathy
2. Personal story of struggle
3. Future consequences of inaction (fear)
4. Hopeful light at the end of the tunnel
5. Teaser for tomorrow's content
6. P.S. (Engagement question)
```

#### Day 7: Sales Launch

**Purpose**: Drive conversions.

**Structure**:
```
Subject: [Launch] + [Limited Offer]
1. Announcement of the offer
2. Details of the offer (value → price)
3. List of bonuses
4. Limited conditions (number/time)
5. How to sign up
6. P.S. (Deadline warning)
```

### Sequence Checklist
- [ ] Cover all six educational elements.
- [ ] Maintain a cycle of fear and hope throughout the sequence.
- [ ] Include teasers for upcoming emails.
- [ ] Gradually increase urgency in the final emails.
- [ ] Ensure every email has a P.S. section.
