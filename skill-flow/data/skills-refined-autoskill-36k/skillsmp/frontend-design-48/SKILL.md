This skill guides creation of distinctive, production-grade frontend interfaces that avoid generic "AI slop" aesthetics.

## Design Direction: Aurora Glass (Dark Mode Moderno)

- **Vibe**: Ethereal, fluid, immersive, tech-forward.
- **Visuals**: 
  - **Background**: Deep dark navy/black (#020205) with slowly moving, blurred "aurora" blobs (Cyan, Magenta, Violet).
  - **Glassmorphism**: Panels and cards are translucent (`rgba(255,255,255,0.03)`) with `backdrop-filter: blur(20px)` and subtle glossy borders.
  - **Typography**: Modern, clean sans-serif (Outfit or Inter). High legibility.
  - **Accents**: Neon glows, gradients that fade into darkness.
- **Motion**: Slow, floating background elements. Smooth hover states that "light up" elements.
