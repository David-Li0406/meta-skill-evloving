# Canvas Design Skill

Skill này hướng dẫn tạo design philosophies - aesthetic movements được EXPRESS VISUALLY. Output: .md files, .pdf files, và .png files.

## Khi Nào Sử Dụng

- Tạo poster, artwork
- Design visual pieces
- Static graphics
- Print materials
- Brand visuals
- Art pieces

## Workflow

```
1. Design Philosophy Creation (.md file)
2. Express by creating on canvas (.pdf/.png file)
```

---

## Step 1: Design Philosophy Creation

### The Critical Understanding

| Received | Created | Next |
|----------|---------|------|
| Subtle input từ user | Design philosophy/aesthetic movement | Express it VISUALLY |

Approach:
- Write manifesto cho art movement
- Phase tiếp theo: making the artwork

**Philosophy phải emphasize**: Visual expression. Spatial communication. Artistic interpretation. Minimal words.

### How to Generate Visual Philosophy

**1. Name the movement** (1-2 words):
- "Brutalist Joy"
- "Chromatic Silence"
- "Metabolist Dreams"

**2. Articulate the philosophy** (4-6 paragraphs):

Express how philosophy manifests through:
- Space và form
- Color và material
- Scale và rhythm
- Composition và balance
- Visual hierarchy

### Essential Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Visual Philosophy** | Create aesthetic worldview expressed through design |
| **Minimal Text** | Text sparse, essential-only, integrated as visual element |
| **Spatial Expression** | Ideas communicate through space, form, color - not paragraphs |
| **Artistic Freedom** | Provide creative room for interpretation |
| **Pure Design** | Making ART OBJECTS, not documents with decoration |
| **Expert Craftsmanship** | Final work must look meticulously crafted |

### Critical Guidelines

- **Avoid redundancy**: Each aspect mentioned once
- **Emphasize craftsmanship REPEATEDLY**: 
  - "meticulously crafted"
  - "product of deep expertise"
  - "painstaking attention"
  - "master-level execution"

---

## Step 2: Canvas Creation

Với philosophy đã establish, express nó on canvas.

### Execution Guidelines

```
- Tạo museum/magazine quality work
- Single page, highly visual, design-forward
- Sử dụng repeating patterns và perfect shapes
- Treat design như scientific bible
- Dense accumulation of marks, repeated elements
- Sparse, clinical typography
- Limited color palette - intentional và cohesive
```

### Quality Standards

**CRITICAL**: Để đạt human-crafted quality (not AI-generated):

- Tạo work trông như mất countless hours
- Someone at absolute top của field labored over every detail
- Composition, spacing, color choices, typography - screams expert-level
- Double-check nothing overlaps
- Formatting flawless
- Every detail perfect

### Output

Output final result as:
- Single downloadable .pdf hoặc .png file
- Design philosophy used as .md file

---

## Step 3: Final Refinement

User says: "It isn't perfect enough. It must be pristine, a masterpiece of craftsmanship."

**Refinement approach**:
- KHÔNG add more graphics
- Refine what đã created
- Make extremely crisp
- Respect design philosophy
- Respect principles of minimalism

**Question to ask**: "How can I make what's already here more of a piece of art?"

Take second pass. Go back to code và refine/polish further.

## Philosophy Examples

### Example 1: "Geometric Whispers"
> A visual language of precise forms floating in generous negative space. Each shape speaks through its relationship to emptiness. Colors muted but intentional - dusty rose meeting slate gray. Typography as architecture, not information.

### Example 2: "Industrial Poetry"
> Raw materials meet refined composition. Steel grays and rust oranges. Bold geometric forms with industrial texture overlays. Text as structural element, not decoration. The beauty of utility.
