# Referral Program: "The Focus Buddy Loop"

## Core Loop
1. **User (Referrer)**: Shares unique invite link with a "Buddy".
2. **Friend (Referee)**: Installs app and gets **1 Month of Pro Free**.
3. **Reward**: Upon friend's first dose log, Referrer earns **1 Grace Day** (streak protection).

## Why this works for ADHD
- **Grace Days**: Highly valued because ADHD users fear losing streaks due to forgetfulness.
- **Reciprocity**: Gifting Pro to a friend reduces the "guilt" of referring.
- **Community**: The mindset of "doing it together" (body doubling) is a proven ADHD coping mechanism.

## Future Milestones
- **3 Referrals**: "Gold Focus Badge" + 3 Months Pro.
- **10 Referrals**: Lifetime access + Member of the "Elite Stackers" community.
