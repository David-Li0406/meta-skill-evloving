# Template: Industry Take / Hot Take Post

## Best rhetoric devices
- **Antithesis** for contrasting positions
- **Anaphora** for building your argument
- **Hyperbaton** for attention-grabbing openings

## Structure

```
[HOOK: Bold claim or controversial statement]

[THE SETUP: Why this matters / context]

[THE ARGUMENT: Your position with evidence]
- Use anaphora here if building momentum
- Or antithesis if contrasting two approaches

[THE UNCOMFORTABLE TRUTH: What nobody wants to admit]

[THE CALL: What should change / what you believe]

[OPTIONAL: Question to spark debate]

#YourIndustry #YourNiche [+ relevant tags]
```

## Example Structure

```
The [industry] has a dirty secret.

They don't want you to [know/learn/do something].

Every [person] who discovers [alternative] is a customer they lose.
Every [team/company] that [makes the switch] is [revenue] they can't collect.
Every success story with [your approach] is a threat to their model.

So they make it complicated.

They [obstacle 1].
They [obstacle 2].
They [obstacle 3].

I'm not saying they're evil. I'm saying their incentives
and your interests are not aligned.

The uncomfortable truth: the best [solution] for your [problem]
might be the one that [unexpected characteristic].

Worth considering before [relevant decision point].

#YourIndustry #YourNiche #RelevantTopic
```

## Variations

### "Myth busting" angle
```
[THE MYTH everyone believes]
[WHY it's wrong]
[THE REALITY]
[WHAT THIS MEANS for you]
```

### "Prediction" angle
```
[WHAT'S CHANGING in the industry]
[WHY it's happening now]
[WHAT THIS MEANS]
[HOW TO PREPARE]
```

### "Contrarian" angle
```
[POPULAR OPINION]
"Actually..." [Your counter-take]
[YOUR EVIDENCE]
[THE NUANCE most people miss]
```

## Voice notes for hot takes

- Take a clear stance - fence-sitting is boring
- Back opinions with experience, not just opinion
- "I could be wrong, but..." is okay occasionally
- Invite disagreement - engagement is the goal
- Don't punch down - criticise systems, not people
- Be provocative, not offensive
- Have evidence or experience to back your claims
