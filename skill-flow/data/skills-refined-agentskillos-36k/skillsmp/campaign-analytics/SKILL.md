## What I do
- Track character appearance frequency across sessions
- Analyze faction relationship dynamics and power shifts
- Identify narrative arcs and plot development patterns
- Generate campaign statistics and content metrics
- Predict future content needs based on story progression
- Map campaign themes and moral axis development

## When to use me
Use this when:
- Planning future sessions and story developments
- Understanding character relationships and alliances
- Analyzing campaign pacing and narrative structure
- Identifying underutilized characters or factions
- Preparing campaign summaries or retrospectives

## Analysis Dimensions

### Character Metrics
- **Appearance Frequency**: How often each character appears
- **Story Impact**: Importance in key plot decisions
- **Relationship Network**: Connections to other characters
- **Development Arc**: Character growth over sessions
- **Survival Rate**: Character longevity in dangerous campaign

### Faction Dynamics
- **Power Balance**: Relative influence between factions
- **Alliance Patterns**: Shifting coalitions and conflicts
- **Political Position**: Stances on key campaign issues
- **Resource Control**: Access to magical or material resources
- **Military Strength**: Defensive and offensive capabilities

### Narrative Patterns
- **Session Pacing**: Action vs. roleplay vs. investigation balance
- **Plot Arc Structure**: Introduction, rising action, climax, resolution
- **Moral Complexity**: Shades of gray in character motivations
- **Threat Escalation**: Increasing danger levels over time
- **Player Agency**: Impact of player decisions on story

### Trend Analysis
1. Calculate character appearance trends
2. Analyze faction power shifts
3. Track moral axis movements (Violence vs. Restraint, etc.)
4. Identify underdeveloped story elements
5. Predict future narrative needs
