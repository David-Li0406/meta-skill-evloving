## Event Types to Prioritize

### High Priority

1. **Limited-time seasonal events** that get us outdoors appreciating our city
   - Example: Seasonal festivals, botanical garden events, outdoor markets

2. **Food events**
   - Night markets
   - One-night-only pop-ups
   - Guest chef appearances
   - Street food festivals

3. **Family-friendly gatherings**
   - Block parties
   - Street closures with stalls and pop-ups
   - Fairs

4. **Museum exhibits**
   - Science themes
   - Interesting art (immersive experiences)

5. **Entertainment**
   - Comedy shows
   - Music (big shows OR local venues)
   - Outdoor movies
   - Top Broadway/theater shows
   - Unique off-Broadway (magic, burlesque, etc.)

6. **Seasonal experiences**
   - Winter pop-ups
   - Summer outdoor venues

### Medium Priority

- Classes and tours (when not too pricey or touristy)
- Reminders of amazing local institutions we take for granted

## Value Multipliers

Events are MORE valuable if they are:

1. **Nearby** - Close to home or easy to get to
2. **Free or reasonably priced**
3. **Great with a toddler/child** AND still fun for adults
   - Casual music in the park
   - Outdoor festivals
   - Kid-friendly but not just-for-kids
4. **Limited time** - Will be gone soon or needs booking immediately
5. **Quintessentially local** - Things we'd miss when we leave
