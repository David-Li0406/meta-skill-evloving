# Skill body

## Core Functions

1. **Health Data Integration**: Collect and integrate data from various health domains including mental health, sleep, fitness, and occupational health.

2. **Trend Analysis**: Analyze trends in health metrics over time, identifying improvements or areas needing attention.
   - **Mental Health**: Track scores from assessments like PHQ-9 and GAD-7.
   - **Sleep**: Analyze sleep duration, quality, and efficiency trends.
   - **Fitness**: Monitor exercise frequency, intensity, and type.
   - **Occupational Health**: Evaluate risks associated with work-related activities.

3. **Pattern Recognition**: Identify patterns in health data to understand correlations and causations.
   - Analyze relationships between mental health and sleep quality.
   - Examine how fitness levels impact mental well-being and sleep patterns.

4. **Personalized Recommendations**: Generate tailored suggestions based on individual health data.
   - **Mental Health**: Provide coping strategies and resources.
   - **Sleep**: Suggest improvements for sleep hygiene and environment.
   - **Fitness**: Recommend exercise routines based on user goals and progress.
   - **Occupational Health**: Offer ergonomic adjustments and work-life balance tips.

5. **Risk Assessment**: Evaluate potential health risks based on user data and provide alerts for high-risk patterns.
   - Identify signs of burnout, sleep disorders, or physical strain from work.

## Execution Steps

### Step 1: Data Collection
- Gather data from various sources:
  - Mental health assessments (e.g., PHQ-9, GAD-7)
  - Sleep tracking logs
  - Fitness activity records
  - Occupational health assessments

### Step 2: Data Analysis
- Perform analyses based on the collected data:
  - **Trend Analysis**: Use statistical methods to identify trends in health metrics.
  - **Correlation Analysis**: Calculate correlation coefficients to understand relationships between different health aspects.

### Step 3: Generate Reports
- Create comprehensive reports summarizing findings, including:
  - Health trends over time
  - Identified patterns and correlations
  - Personalized recommendations for improvement

### Step 4: Provide Recommendations
- Based on the analysis, deliver actionable recommendations tailored to the user's health needs.

### Step 5: Follow-Up
- Encourage users to track their progress and reassess their health data periodically to adjust recommendations as needed.

## Trigger Conditions
This skill is activated when users request:
- Comprehensive health assessments
- Trend analysis across health domains
- Personalized wellness recommendations
- Risk assessments based on health data

## Safety Boundaries
- This skill does not provide medical diagnoses or prescriptions.
- It is not a substitute for professional medical advice or treatment.
- Users are encouraged to consult healthcare professionals for serious health concerns.
