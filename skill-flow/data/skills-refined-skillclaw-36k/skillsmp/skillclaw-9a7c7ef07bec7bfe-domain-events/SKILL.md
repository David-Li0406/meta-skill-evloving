# Domain Events

This skill provides guidelines and best practices for implementing domain events in the context of Domain-Driven Design (DDD). 

1. **Understand Domain Events**: Recognize that domain events are significant occurrences within your domain model that indicate a change in state.
2. **Identify Events**: Determine which events are relevant to your domain and should be modeled as domain events.
3. **Define Event Structure**: Create a clear structure for your domain events, including necessary attributes and metadata.
4. **Publish Events**: Implement a mechanism to publish domain events to interested subscribers, ensuring that they are processed asynchronously.
5. **Handle Events**: Design event handlers that respond to domain events, updating the state of your application or triggering other processes as needed.
6. **Test Events**: Ensure that your domain events and their handlers are thoroughly tested to maintain the integrity of your domain model.

For detailed documentation, refer to the guidelines provided in the relevant resources.
