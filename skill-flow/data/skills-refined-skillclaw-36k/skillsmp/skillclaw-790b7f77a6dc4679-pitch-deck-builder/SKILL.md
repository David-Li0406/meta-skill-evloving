# Skill body

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:
- Create a pitch deck for fundraising purposes.
- Present your business idea to potential investors.
- Optimize an existing pitch deck for better engagement and clarity.

**Not recommended for:**
- Tasks requiring extensive creative design work.
- Technical coding or software development.

## Quick Reference

| Action | Command/Trigger |
|--------|-----------------|
| Create pitch deck | `pitch deck` |
| Review and optimize | `review pitch deck` |
| Get best practices | `pitch deck best practices` |

## Core Workflows

### Workflow 1: Initial Pitch Deck Creation

**Goal:** Create a high-quality pitch deck from scratch.

**Steps:**
1. **Discovery** - Understand the requirements and objectives of your pitch.
2. **Planning** - Develop a strategy and outline for your pitch deck.
3. **Execution** - Create the pitch deck based on the plan.
4. **Review** - Evaluate the pitch deck and gather feedback.
5. **Optimization** - Refine the pitch deck based on feedback and best practices.

### Workflow 2: Advanced Pitch Deck Optimization

**Goal:** Refine and optimize an existing pitch deck for better results.

**Steps:**
1. **Research** - Gather relevant information and examples of successful pitch decks.
2. **Analysis** - Evaluate the current pitch deck and identify areas for improvement.
3. **Decision** - Choose the best strategies for enhancement.
4. **Implementation** - Execute the changes to the pitch deck.
5. **Measurement** - Track the effectiveness of the updated pitch deck.

## Best Practices

1. **Start with Clear Objectives** - Define what success looks like for your pitch.
2. **Follow Proven Frameworks** - Utilize established structures and best practices in pitch deck creation.
3. **Iterate Based on Feedback** - Continuously improve your pitch deck based on input from peers and mentors.
4. **Document Your Process** - Keep a record of decisions and changes made during the creation process.
5. **Focus on Quality** - Prioritize clarity and professionalism in your pitch deck design and content.

## Checklist

Before considering your pitch deck complete:

- [ ] Objectives clearly defined and understood.
- [ ] Research and discovery phase completed.
- [ ] Strategy or plan documented.
- [ ] Implementation matches requirements.
- [ ] Feedback incorporated and revisions made.
