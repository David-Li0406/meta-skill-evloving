Large-format floor installation

Overview

Large-format porcelain floor installation across an open-plan living area, kitchen, and hallway in a modern coastal home. The project required flawless leveling across 85 square meters, with tiles running continuously between spaces.

Working with rectified 900×900mm tiles, we achieved a seamless finish with minimal grout lines and perfectly flat surfaces throughout.

Floor installation in progress

Leveling system ensures perfectly flat large-format tiles

The challenge

Large-format tiles present unique challenges. At 900×900mm, even minor substrate variations become visible lippage issues. The open-plan design meant tiles needed to flow seamlessly between rooms with different natural light exposures.

The substrate had multiple height variations from previous renovations, requiring extensive preparation before any tiling could begin.

Installation approach

01 / Foundation

Substrate leveling

Spent two full days leveling the substrate using self-leveling compound and spot repairs. Laser level used throughout to ensure consistent heights across all areas.

02 / Layout

Dry layout planning

Planned tile layout to minimize cuts and ensure symmetry in high-visibility areas. Grout lines aligned between rooms for visual continuity.

03 / Installation

Precision tiling

Used premium flexible adhesive with mechanical leveling clips on every tile. Progress checked continuously with straight-edges to prevent any lippage.

04 / Completion

Grouting & sealing

Applied color-matched grout with minimal joint width (2mm). All tiles sealed with penetrating sealer for long-term protection and easy maintenance.

The result

A flawless large-format floor installation with zero lippage issues and perfect alignment throughout. The tiles flow seamlessly between spaces, creating a sophisticated and expansive feel in the home.

The client was particularly impressed with the attention to substrate preparation, which ensured the long-term durability and appearance of the installation.
