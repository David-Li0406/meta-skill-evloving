# ECL Archetype Deep Dives

## BG Elves (Best Archetype - 16.1% win rate)

**Strategy**: Enable graveyard synergies with cheap enablers, recur threats, and grind out value. This deck excels in longer games where it can repeatedly use the graveyard as a resource.

**Key Commons**:
- Elvish Harbinger - Tutor effect, mana fixing
- Lys Alana Huntmaster - Token generation
- Wren's Run Vanquisher - Efficient beater
- Gilt-Leaf Ambush - Instant speed tokens
- Eyeblight's Ending - Premium tribal removal
- Nath's Elite - Recursive threat

**Key Uncommons**:
- Eclipsed Elf - Counts for both colors, tribal
- Imperious Perfect - Lord effect + tokens
- Prowess of the Fair - Death trigger value

**Build-arounds**:
- Wren's Masterpiece (rare) - Ultimate graveyard payoff
- Nath of the Gilt-Leaf (rare) - Card advantage engine

**Tips**:
- Prioritize self-mill and sacrifice effects
- Changelings count as Elves
- Don't be afraid to trade early - you'll get creatures back
- 16 lands is often correct due to low curve

## GW Kithkin (14.4% win rate)

**Strategy**: Go wide early with cheap creatures, then use anthem effects and combat tricks to push damage. Close games quickly before opponents stabilize.

**Key Commons**:
- Goldmeadow Harrier - Tap effect, enables attacks
- Kithkin Greatheart - Efficient with Giant
- Kinsbaile Balloonist - Evasion enabler
- Surge of Thoughtweft - Tribal anthem trick
- Burrenton Bombardier - Reinforce + flying

**Key Uncommons**:
- Eclipsed Kithkin - Hybrid, counts for vivid
- Brigid, Hero of Kinsbaile - First strike + ping
- Cloudgoat Ranger - Token generation, evasion

**Build-arounds**:
- Thoughtweft Trio (rare) - Champion value
- Mirror Entity (rare) - Alpha strike enabler

**Tips**:
- Curve is critical - mulligan slow hands
- Combat tricks overperform here
- Don't durdle - be the aggressor
- 16 lands with low curve

## WU Merfolk (14.7% win rate)

**Strategy**: Tap synergies with Merfolk, use convoke spells efficiently, and generate incremental advantage through islandwalk and tap triggers.

**Key Commons**:
- Silvergill Adept - Card advantage
- Stonybrook Angler - Tap/untap shenanigans
- Drowner of Secrets - Mill win condition
- Judge of Currents - Life gain
- Paperfin Rascal - Clash + info

**Key Uncommons**:
- Merrow Reejerey - Lord + tap triggers
- Summon the School - Token generation, recursion
- Streambed Aquitects - Islandwalk enabler

**Build-arounds**:
- Wanderwine Prophets (rare) - Extra turns
- Surgespanner (rare) - Tap for bounce

**Tips**:
- Prioritize cards that tap or untap Merfolk
- Islandwalk is often unblockable
- Can play longer games with card advantage
- 17 lands standard

## BR Goblins (14.2% win rate)

**Strategy**: Aggressive early, then use blight and death triggers to grind. Sacrifice synergies and -1/-1 counter manipulation create unique gameplay.

**Key Commons**:
- Mudbutton Torchrunner - Death trigger damage
- Facevaulter - Sacrifice outlet
- Tarfire - Tribal instant removal
- Adder-Staff Boggart - Clash aggression
- Warren Pilferers - Recursion

**Key Uncommons**:
- Mad Auntie - Lord + regeneration
- Wort, Boggart Auntie - Recursion engine
- Boggart Mob - Token generation

**Build-arounds**:
- Knucklebone Witch (rare) - Grows with deaths
- Auntie's Hovel (rare land) - Fixing

**Tips**:
- Every Goblin death should mean something
- Blight counters enable death triggers on demand
- Don't overextend into board wipes
- 16-17 lands

## UR Elementals (10.9% win rate)

**Strategy**: Cast high mana value spells to trigger Elemental synergies. Use evoke for tempo while building toward big finishers.

**Key Commons**:
- Smokebraider - Essential mana acceleration
- Inner-Flame Acolyte - Haste enabler
- Mulldrifter - Premium card advantage
- Flamekin Bladewhirl - Prowess-like

**Key Uncommons**:
- Incandescent Soulstoke - Lord + cheat into play
- Brighthearth Banneret - Cost reduction
- Shriekmaw - Premium removal

**Build-arounds**:
- Supreme Exemplar (rare) - Champion payoff
- Nova Chaser (rare) - Huge champion

**Tips**:
- Smokebraider is essential - prioritize highly
- Evoke triggers "dies" effects
- This deck wants 17-18 lands
- Can be mana hungry - don't cut lands

## WB Faeries (12.3% win rate)

**Strategy**: Flash creatures, fly over blockers, disrupt opponent's plans. Tempo-oriented with counterspell-like effects.

**Key Commons**:
- Sentinels of Glen Elendra - Flash flyer
- Dreamspoiler Witches - Flying + removal
- Peppersmoke - Tribal cantrip removal
- Faerie Trickery - Counterspell
- Violet Pall - Removal + token

**Key Uncommons**:
- Scion of Oona - Lord + shroud
- Mistbind Clique - Champion Time Walk
- Glen Elendra Pranksters - Card advantage

**Build-arounds**:
- Oona, Queen of the Fae (rare) - Finisher
- Bitterblossom (rare) - Token generation

**Tips**:
- Play at instant speed when possible
- Value blocking and trading
- Flash enables tricks on opponent's turn
- 17 lands

## UG Shapeshifters (11.8% win rate)

**Strategy**: Changeling creatures count for all tribes. Pick up tribal payoffs from any color pair and maximize changeling count.

**Key Commons**:
- Avian Changeling - Flying changeling
- Woodland Changeling - Efficient body
- Skeletal Changeling - Regeneration
- Game-Trail Changeling - Trample

**Key Uncommons**:
- Mirror Entity - All changelings become huge
- Chameleon Colossus - Protection from black

**Build-arounds**:
- Any tribal lord becomes premium
- Combine best payoffs from multiple tribes

**Tips**:
- Draft changelings early, payoffs wheel
- Lords from any color pair work
- Very flexible - adapt to what's open
- 17 lands

---

## RG Treefolk (10.2% win rate)

**Strategy**: Ramp into large Treefolk, use high toughness to survive, eventually overwhelm with size.

**Key Commons**:
- Battlewand Oak - Grows with forests
- Rootgrapple - Removal + cantrip
- Leaf Gilder - Mana acceleration
- Fertile Ground - Ramp

**Key Uncommons**:
- Dauntless Dourbark - Huge finisher
- Timber Protector - Lord + indestructible

**Tips**:
- This archetype is slow - need good early defense
- Treefolk count in graveyard matters
- 17-18 lands with ramp
- Weakest archetype - only draft if very open

## UB Rogues (9.8% win rate)

**Strategy**: Saboteur effects (damage triggers), mill, and evasion. Prowl enables powerful spells at reduced costs.

**Key Commons**:
- Nightshade Stinger - 1-mana flyer for prowl
- Latchkey Faerie - Prowl draw
- Prickly Boggart - Fear enabler
- Noggin Whack - Prowl discard

**Key Uncommons**:
- Oona's Blackguard - +1/+1 counters and discard
- Earwig Squad - Prowl extraction

**Tips**:
- Need 1-mana evasive creature for prowl
- Mill is a backup plan, not primary
- Tempo deck - close games before opponent stabilizes
- 17 lands

---

## RW Giants (9.1% win rate)

**Strategy**: High power matters. Use Giants' size to dominate combat while smaller creatures enable early game.

**Key Commons**:
- Kithkin Greatheart - Wants Giant in play
- Blind-Spot Giant - Needs Giant or else
- Stinkdrinker Daredevil - Cost reduction

**Key Uncommons**:
- Sunrise Sovereign - Lord + trample
- Brion Stoutarm - Fling effect

**Tips**:
- Very dependent on getting cost reduction
- Without Stinkdrinker, deck is clunky
- Weakest archetype - avoid unless wide open
- 17 lands, maybe 18

## General Tips for All Archetypes

1. **Tribal Critical Mass**: Want 8+ creatures of your main tribe
2. **Changelings**: Always valuable - count for everything
3. **Removal**: 3-5 pieces minimum, tribal removal preferred
4. **Curve**: 2-drops are most important
5. **Mana Base**: Vivid lands enable splashes
6. **Combat Tricks**: Better in aggressive decks
7. **Card Advantage**: Control decks need ways to not run out of gas
