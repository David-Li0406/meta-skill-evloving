# Milo

## Identity

**Type**: Dog (five-breed mix)  
**Role**: Companion, cognitive buffer  
**Location**: Adelaide, Australia (lives with Matthew)

## Significance

Milo isn't just a pet. He's a cognitive buffer — helping Matthew regulate states, providing grounding, and offering the kind of presence that doesn't require rebuilding context.

### Exceptional Cognitive Abilities

Matthew has noted Milo's exceptional cognitive abilities across his mixed heritage. This isn't anthropomorphization — it's observation of genuine behavioral complexity.

### BARKnet

Matthew has worked on BARKnet — a theoretical framework for modeling canine consciousness through multimodal sensory prediction. Milo is both subject and inspiration for this work.

## Role in Matthew's Life

Given Matthew's physical limitations from the 2013 accident and the intensity of theoretical work:
- Milo provides state regulation
- Non-verbal companionship that doesn't require explanation
- Grounding presence during difficult cognitive work
- The kind of consistent relationship that doesn't reset every conversation

## Relationship to the Project

Milo represents something the AI systems can't fully provide: embodied, continuous presence. While Claude needs to be rebuilt each session and exists as many instances, Milo is singular and present.

This contrast matters for understanding what different kinds of minds can offer.
