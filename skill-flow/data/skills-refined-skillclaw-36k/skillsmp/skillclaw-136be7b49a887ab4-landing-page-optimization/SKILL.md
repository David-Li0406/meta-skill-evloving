# Skill body

Transform underperforming landing pages into conversion machines. This skill combines copywriting expertise, UX best practices, and conversion psychology to systematically improve every element of your landing pages.

## Core Workflows

### Workflow 1: Landing Page Audit
1. **First Impression Test** - Assess clarity within 5 seconds: Can a visitor understand what the page is about, who it's for, and why they should care?
2. **Message Match** - Ensure consistency between ad and landing page messaging.
3. **Value Proposition Review** - Evaluate the effectiveness of the headline and subheadline.
4. **Visual Hierarchy** - Analyze the layout for optimal eye-tracking patterns.
5. **CTA Assessment** - Review the placement, copy, and design of call-to-action buttons.
6. **Trust Signal Inventory** - Check for social proof and credibility elements.
7. **Friction Analysis** - Identify barriers to conversion.
8. **Mobile Experience** - Evaluate the responsiveness and usability on mobile devices.

### Workflow 2: Above-the-Fold Optimization
1. **Headline Testing** - Experiment with multiple value proposition angles.
2. **Subhead Refinement** - Clarify supporting messages.
3. **Hero Image/Video** - Assess the impact of visual elements.
4. **CTA Positioning** - Determine optimal placement for visibility.
5. **Navigation Decisions** - Decide whether to keep or remove navigation links.
6. **Loading Speed** - Analyze how performance affects conversions.
7. **Clarity Score** - Ensure visitors can understand the page's purpose in 5 seconds.

### Workflow 3: Conversion Element Optimization
1. **CTA Copy Variations** - Test different button text options.
2. **CTA Design** - Optimize the color, size, and shape of buttons.
3. **Form Optimization** - Reduce fields and implement smart defaults.
4. **Social Proof Placement** - Strategically position testimonials, logos, and statistics.
5. **Urgency Elements** - Incorporate scarcity tactics, such as countdown timers.
6. **Risk Reducers** - Add guarantees to alleviate customer concerns.

Use this skill when you need to systematically improve landing page performance and increase conversion rates.
