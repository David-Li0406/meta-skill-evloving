# Aggregates and Aggregate Roots

This skill provides guidelines and best practices for working with aggregates and aggregate roots in Domain-Driven Design (DDD). 

1. **Understand the Concept**: Familiarize yourself with the definitions of aggregates and aggregate roots.
2. **Identify Boundaries**: Determine the boundaries of your aggregates based on business requirements.
3. **Design Aggregate Roots**: Ensure that each aggregate root is responsible for maintaining the integrity of its aggregate.
4. **Implement Invariants**: Define and enforce invariants within your aggregates to maintain consistency.
5. **Use Repositories**: Utilize repositories to manage the lifecycle of aggregates, ensuring proper retrieval and storage.
6. **Follow DDD Principles**: Adhere to Domain-Driven Design principles throughout your implementation.

For detailed documentation, refer to the guidelines in the relevant resources.
