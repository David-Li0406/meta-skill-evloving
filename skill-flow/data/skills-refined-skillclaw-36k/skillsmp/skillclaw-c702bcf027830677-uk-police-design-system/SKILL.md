## Domain Context

### The UK Police Recruitment Landscape

This system serves Peel Solutions, which provides managed investigator teams to UK police forces. Key entities include:

- 43 territorial police forces in England & Wales
- Regional Organised Crime Units (ROCUs)
- National Crime Agency (NCA)
- British Transport Police and specialist forces

### Target Roles

- Police Investigators (PIP2 accredited)
- Intelligence Analysts
- Digital Forensic Examiners
- Major Crime Investigators
- Surveillance Officers

## Design Philosophy: Technical Luxury

"Technical Luxury" embodies the fusion of precision engineering and high-end elegance. Key principles include:

- Every pixel earns its place.
- Speed as a feature (sub-100ms interactions).
- Determinism (predictable behavior, consistent locations).
- Professional gravitas appropriate for the policing context.

### Why Dark Mode First

1. **Reduced eye strain** during extended use.
2. Enhanced focus and clarity in data presentation.
3. Improved battery life for mobile devices.
