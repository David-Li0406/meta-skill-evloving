# Skill body

## Step 1: Design Philosophy Creation (.md file)

1. **Create a Visual Philosophy**: Develop a unique design philosophy that will guide your visual expression. This should not be a layout or template but a conceptual framework.
   
   - **Name the Movement** (1-2 words): Examples include "Brutalist Joy," "Chromatic Silence," or "Metabolist Dreams."
   
   - **Articulate the Philosophy** (4-6 paragraphs):
     - Describe how the philosophy manifests through:
       - Space and form
       - Color and material
       - Scale and rhythm
       - Composition and balance
       - Visual hierarchy
     - Emphasize craftsmanship, ensuring the final work appears meticulously crafted and the product of deep expertise.

2. **Output the Philosophy**: Save your design philosophy in a .md file.

## Step 2: Express the Philosophy Visually (.pdf or .png file)

1. **Create Visual Art**: Using your design philosophy, create a visual representation that embodies the concepts outlined. Focus on:
   - Form, space, color, and composition
   - Images, graphics, shapes, and patterns
   - Minimal text as a visual accent

2. **Output the Visual Art**: Save your artwork as a .pdf or .png file.

### Critical Guidelines:
- Avoid redundancy in your descriptions; each design aspect should be mentioned once.
- Ensure that the final work is original and does not copy existing artists' work to avoid copyright violations.
