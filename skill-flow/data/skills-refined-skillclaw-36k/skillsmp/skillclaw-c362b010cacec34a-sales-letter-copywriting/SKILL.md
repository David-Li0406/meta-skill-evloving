# Skill body

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill in the following scenarios:
- When you need to write a sales letter or landing page.
- When you want to create compelling copy that converts.
- When you need to structure your sales message effectively.

## Key Components of a Sales Letter

1. **Head Copy (Catchphrase)**
   - Create an attention-grabbing headline that hooks the reader.

2. **Problem Recognition**
   - Identify the current situation and the problems your audience faces.

3. **Solution Presentation**
   - Clearly present your product or service as the solution to the identified problems.

4. **Benefits and Future Vision**
   - Highlight the benefits of your product and paint a picture of the positive future it offers.

5. **Proof and Evidence**
   - Provide testimonials, case studies, or statistics to back up your claims.

6. **Product Details**
   - Describe what the product is and what it includes.

7. **Price Presentation**
   - Clearly state the price and justify it with logical reasoning.

8. **Bonuses**
   - Offer additional incentives or bonuses to encourage purchase.

9. **Guarantee and Support**
   - Include any guarantees or support options to reduce buyer hesitation.

10. **Call to Action (CTA)**
    - End with a clear and compelling call to action, prompting the reader to take the next step.

11. **Postscript (P.S.)**
    - Reinforce the main point or offer in a postscript to capture attention again.

## Writing Guidelines

- **Emotional Engagement**: Use language that evokes emotions and connects with the reader.
- **Logical Justification**: Ensure that the price and benefits are presented logically.
- **Clarity**: Be specific about the benefits and features of your product.
- **Clear CTA**: Make sure the call to action is straightforward and easy to follow.

## Customization Based on Product Price

### High-Priced Products (30万円以上)
- Build authority through a four-layer structure (achievements, experience, knowledge, personality).
- Use explanatory phrases (のです/なのです) 30% of the time.
- Offer a 90-day money-back guarantee and installment payment options.

### Mid-Priced Products (10-30万円)
- Include at least three specific success stories.
- Quantify cost-effectiveness (e.g., 10,000% ROI).
- Use contrast stories (traditional vs. your product).

### Low-Priced Products (10万円以下)
- Use short sentences (52% short sentences, 12% exclamatory sentences).
- Emphasize immediate usability.
- Increase the frequency of bullet points.

## Checklist for Writing

### Before Writing
- [ ] Define your target audience.
- [ ] Choose a pricing strategy based on product category.
- [ ] Prepare five stories and collect evidence (images, case studies).

### During Writing
- [ ] Maintain a balance of sentence lengths (short, medium, long).
- [ ] Ensure emotional engagement through fear-hope cycles.
- [ ] Include evidence for each claim.

### After Writing
- [ ] Read aloud to check for flow.
- [ ] Ensure clarity and correctness in all claims.
- [ ] Verify that the CTA is clear and compelling.
